# Square — 1080x1080

**Use for:** Instagram feed, LinkedIn, universal social

---

## Layout

- Padding: 48-56px all sides
- Compact — every pixel matters
- Center everything, minimal whitespace
- Single focal point per scene

## Typography

- Hero headline: 36-44px
- Section headline: 28-34px
- Body: 16-20px
- Labels: 12-13px

## Illustrations

- Scale down 0.7-0.9x compared to portrait
- Keep tight to text

## Pacing

- Short — 15-25s max
- Fewer scenes (4-5)
- Faster transitions (12 frame overlap)

## Notes

- Works on both mobile and desktop feeds
- Text must be readable at small sizes (feed preview)
- Avoid fine details in illustrations — they'll be small
